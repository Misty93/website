About the data source

Fresh Phish is an interface built on top of the Phishing.Database project. This page automatically retrieves publicly available phishing intelligence feeds and presents them in a searchable format for educational and threat intelligence purposes.

The Phishing.Database project was created and is maintained by Mitchell Krog and Nissar Chababy, together with numerous contributors and Community Safekeepers who continuously validate new phishing reports and maintain the quality of the dataset.

The project is an official threat intelligence data vendor for VirusTotal and is built around the principle that phishing intelligence should remain free, open and accessible to everyone.

Huge thanks to the entire Phishing.Database team for making this resource freely available to the cybersecurity community.
